Text-to-Speech Technology

Understanding how text is converted to natural-sounding speech

Overview

TextSense is powered by OpenAI's advanced text-to-speech models for natural-sounding voice synthesis. These models generate high-quality speech from text with support for multiple voices and custom emotion/style prompts.

Voice Options

The text-to-speech service offers multiple AI voices, each with unique characteristics:

  • Alloy - Balanced and clear voice
  • Echo - Resonant and expressive
  • Fable - Storytelling tone
  • Onyx - Deep and authoritative
  • Nova - Bright and energetic
  • Shimmer - Soft and gentle

Customization Features

  • Emotion Prompts: Customize the emotional tone of the speech (happy, sad, excited, etc.)
  • Style Adaptation: Adjust speaking style for different contexts
  • High Quality: Advanced neural networks ensure natural-sounding output
  • Format Support: Generate audio in MP3 format for easy download and sharing
