Privacy Policy

Transparency about data we process and why

Information We Process

We do not store any information. Your submitted text and uploaded files are processed temporarily only for the purpose of providing analysis results.

  • Submitted text and uploaded files are processed in real-time to perform analysis
  • No data is stored on our servers or any third-party services
  • All processing happens in memory and is immediately discarded after results are returned

How We Use Your Data

  • To analyze text and return detection results in real-time
  • Data is processed temporarily and never retained for any other purpose

Data Retention

We retain zero data. Submitted text and uploaded files are processed in memory and immediately discarded after analysis. No information is stored, logged, or cached in any form.

GDPR and CCPA

If you are located in the EU, UK, or California, you may have rights to access or delete your personal information. Contact us at hi@textsense.com to make a request.

Third Parties

For inference, we may relay to a Hugging Face Space. That service processes your submitted text solely to provide analysis.
