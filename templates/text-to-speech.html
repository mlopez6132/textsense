How to Use
Getting Started
  • Enter Text: Type or paste your text in the input area
  • Choose Voice: Select from 6 different AI voices
  • Add Emotion/Style: Optionally describe how you want it spoken (e.g., "excitedly", "calmly")
  • Generate: Click 'Generate Speech' to create audio
Tips & Best Practices
  • Keep text under 5000 characters for best results
  • Use proper punctuation for natural speech pauses
  • Different voices work better for different content types
  • Emotion/style prompts customize the delivery (e.g., "whispering mysteriously", "shouting excitedly")
