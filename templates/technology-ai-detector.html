AI Detector Technology

Understanding the technology behind AI text detection

Overview

TextSense uses the Desklib AI Text Detector, a fine-tuned model based on microsoft/deberta-v3-large. This advanced transformer model aggregates token embeddings with mean pooling and applies a classifier head to output an AI probability score for each sentence.

Architecture

  • Transformer Base: DeBERTa v3 Large - A state-of-the-art transformer architecture developed by Microsoft
  • Mean Pooling: Aggregates attention-masked hidden states using mean pooling for robust feature extraction
  • Classifier Head: Applies a classifier head to output AI probability scores
  • Sentence-Level Analysis: Provides probability scores via sigmoid activation on classifier logits for each sentence

How It Works

The AI detector processes text through several stages:

  1. Tokenization: The input text is tokenized using DeBERTa's tokenizer
  2. Embedding: Token embeddings are generated through the transformer layers
  3. Pooling: Hidden states are aggregated using mean pooling with attention masking
  4. Classification: The pooled features are passed through a classifier to produce AI probability scores
  5. Visualization: Results are displayed with sentence-level highlighting and probability scores
