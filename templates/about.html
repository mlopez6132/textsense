Mission

TextSense empowers educators, researchers, and creators with transparent, sentence-level insights into AI-generated text. We aim to promote responsible AI use by providing clear, actionable analysis.

Vision

We envision a world where humans and AI collaborate effectively—supported by tools that make provenance and originality visible, fair, and easy to evaluate.

Our Story

TextSense was created after observing the growing need for precise, sentence-level detection that goes beyond binary labels. Our team built a fast, user-friendly detector with detailed highlighting to help people understand where and why content looks AI-generated.
