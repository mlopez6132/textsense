About TextSense

Our mission, vision, and the story behind TextSense

Mission

TextSense provides free, open text analysis tools for everyone. We believe that powerful text processing capabilities should be accessible without paywalls or subscriptions. Our mission is to democratize text analysis technology and make professional-grade tools available to students, researchers, content creators, and professionals worldwide.

Our Tools

We offer a comprehensive suite of text analysis tools:

  • AI Text Detector: Advanced sentence-level detection of AI-generated content with visual highlighting
  • Image to Text (OCR): Extract text from images with high accuracy
  • Audio to Text: Convert speech to text with AI-powered transcription
  • Text to Image: Generate images from text descriptions using AI

Our Values

  • Free Access: All tools are completely free with no paywalls or subscriptions
  • Privacy Focused: We don't store any of your data - complete privacy guaranteed
  • Easy to Use: Simple, fast tools accessible to everyone without registration
  • High Quality: Professional-grade results you can trust
