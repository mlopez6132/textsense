How to Use
Upload Methods
  • Drag & Drop: Simply drag your image into the upload area
  • Click to Browse: Click the upload area to select files
  • Paste from Clipboard: Use Ctrl+V (Cmd+V on Mac)
  • Image URL: Paste a direct link to an online image
Best Results Tips
  • Use high-resolution images
  • Ensure good contrast between text and background
  • Avoid blurry or tilted images
  • Works best with printed text
Technology

Image-to-text extraction is powered by PaddleOCR running on the server. It performs text detection and recognition with angle classification for robust results.

  • Pipeline: angle classification → text detection → text recognition
  • Optimized for CPU inference; starts quickly and is lightweight
  • Outputs are combined in natural reading order
